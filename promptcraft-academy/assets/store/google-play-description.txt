SIX LEARNING TRACKS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🧠 PROMPT ENGINEERING
AI literacy is the new reading literacy. PromptCraft Academy teaches children how to communicate with AI tools clearly and creatively — forming precise questions, iterating on ideas, and evaluating responses critically. These skills transfer directly to school, future careers, and everyday problem-solving.

Levels 1–10 progress from simple single-step prompts all the way to multi-part creative briefs. By Level 10, kids are writing prompts that produce genuinely impressive results — and they understand *why* they work.

✍️ CREATIVE WRITING
Every child has stories to tell. Our Creative Writing track helps them tell them better. AI-powered story starters, character generators, setting builders, and genre challenge modes combine with structured writing lessons based on craft principles used by real authors.

Children build a personal story portfolio as they work through the track. By the end, they'll have completed short stories, poems, dialogue scenes, and a full story outline they're proud of.

💻 CODING FUNDAMENTALS
The best time to introduce coding concepts is before kids develop the fear of getting it wrong. PromptCraft Academy's Coding track uses logic puzzles and story-driven sequence challenges to teach computational thinking without requiring any prior experience or setup.

Kids learn variables, conditions, loops, and debugging — not by memorising syntax, but by solving creative problems. The skills transfer seamlessly to Scratch, Python, and beyond.

📖 STORYTELLING & NARRATIVE
Strong communicators stand out in every field. This track teaches the craft behind great stories: three-act structure, character development, show-don't-tell, dialogue punctuation, pacing, and narrative voice.

Each lesson combines guided reading of exemplar texts, practice exercises with AI feedback, and open-ended creative challenges. Kids develop genuine analytical and creative writing skills aligned to school English curricula.

🔢 MATH PROBLEM-SOLVING
Maths anxiety is real — and word problems are often where it starts. PromptCraft Academy transforms word problems into adventure scenarios, then uses AI to walk kids through multiple solution approaches step by step.

Kids learn to break down complex problems, identify what information matters, check their reasoning, and discover that there's almost always more than one valid path to the answer. Builds the mindset, not just the mechanics.

🔬 SCIENCE EXPLORATION
Curiosity is the engine of science. This track puts children in the role of researcher: forming hypotheses, designing simple experiments, analysing results, and asking the follow-up questions that keep inquiry going.

AI acts as a knowledgeable lab partner — answering questions, suggesting variables to test, and helping kids understand why results sometimes surprise us. Aligned to NGSS (US) and UK KS2/KS3 Science.

SAFETY & PRIVACY — OUR PROMISE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

We built PromptCraft Academy as parents ourselves. Safety is not a feature — it is the foundation.

NO ADVERTISING: PromptCraft Academy has never shown an advertisement and never will. Our only revenue comes from subscriptions paid by parents. Your child's attention is not our product.

NO SOCIAL FEATURES: There are no public profiles, no comment sections, no follower counts, no direct messaging between children, and no ability for strangers to contact your child in any way.

CHILD-SAFE AI: Every single AI response generated in the App passes through a proprietary multi-layer child-safety filter before it reaches a young user. Inappropriate content is blocked and logged for review. The filter is tested and updated monthly.

COPPA COMPLIANT: We comply fully with the US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act. No personal information is collected from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

GDPR-K COMPLIANT: We meet the highest EU and UK standards for children's data protection, including the UK Children's Code and GDPR Article 8.

DATA MINIMISATION: We collect only what we absolutely need to operate the App. We do not collect GPS location, advertising identifiers, or any biometric data.

NO DATA SALES: Your child's data is never sold, rented, or shared with any third party for commercial purposes. Full stop.

FULL DELETION: Delete your child's account and all associated data at any time — instantly, with no penalty and no questions asked.

SOC 2 CERTIFIED: Our infrastructure is hosted on AWS with SOC 2 Type II certification and AES-256 encryption at rest.
