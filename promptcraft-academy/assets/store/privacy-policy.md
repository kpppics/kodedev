# Privacy Policy — PromptCraft Academy

**Effective date:** March 27, 2026
**Last updated:** March 27, 2026

PromptCraft Academy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your child's data.

This policy applies to the PromptCraft Academy mobile application (the "App") and the website at www.promptcraft.academy (the "Site").

---

## 1. Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

PromptCraft Academy is designed for children ages 6–16. We take our obligations under the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**, the **EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, the **UK Children's Code**, and **GDPR-K** (children-specific data protection standards) extremely seriously.

We collect the **minimum data necessary** to operate the App. We do not display advertising. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We never have, and never will.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2a. Information Collected from Parents or Legal Guardians

When a parent or guardian creates an account on behalf of a child, we collect:

- **Email address** — used to send account confirmations, weekly progress digests, and important account notices.
- **Name** (optional) — used to personalise communications.
- **Payment information** — if you subscribe to a Premium or Family plan, payment is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We never see or store your full card number.

### 2b. Information Collected About Children

After parental consent is obtained, we collect a limited profile for each child:

- **First name or nickname** — to personalise the in-app experience (e.g., "Good morning, Alex!"). A real name is not required; a nickname or initials work equally well.
- **Age or year-group** — used to select age-appropriate content difficulty.
- **Optional profile photo** — stored securely and never used outside the App. The child's face is never used for facial recognition or biometric processing of any kind.

### 2c. Usage Data

We collect anonymised, aggregated usage data to improve the App. This includes:

- Which learning tracks and challenges are used most frequently.
- Session length and frequency.
- Badge and achievement milestones.
- Crash reports and technical error logs.

This data cannot be used to identify any individual child. It is used solely to improve the educational experience for all users.

### 2d. AI Prompt Data

Children type or speak prompts into the App as part of learning activities. These prompts:

- Are processed by our AI provider to generate educational responses.
- Are filtered through a strict child-safety content layer before any response is returned.
- Are retained for up to **30 days** to enable features such as "continue where you left off."
- Are **never used to train AI models** without explicit, separate, opt-in parental consent (which we do not currently request).
- Are **never reviewed by humans** except in the case of a reported safety concern, in which case a trained child-safety reviewer may examine the specific prompt in question.

### 2e. Device Information

We collect limited technical information to ensure the App functions correctly:

- Device type and operating system version.
- App version number.
- A randomly generated, non-identifiable device token for push notification delivery.

We do **not** collect precise GPS location, advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID), or any biometric data.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above **only** for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Lawful Basis |
|---|---|
| Providing the App and its educational features | Performance of contract |
| Sending weekly progress digests to parents | Legitimate interest / consent |
| Processing subscription payments | Performance of contract |
| Improving App content and fixing bugs | Legitimate interest (anonymised data only) |
| Responding to support requests | Legitimate interest |
| Complying with legal obligations | Legal obligation |
| Detecting and preventing abuse or safety violations | Legitimate interest / legal obligation |

We do **not** use personal data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

---

## 4. Parental Consent Process

### How We Obtain Consent

In compliance with COPPA (applicable to users in the United States), we require **verifiable parental consent** before collecting any personal information from children under 13.

When a parent registers, we:

1. Clearly explain what data we collect and why.
2. Provide a link to this Privacy Policy in plain language.
3. Ask the parent to confirm they are the legal guardian of the child creating an account.
4. Send a confirmation email to the parent's email address, which must be verified before the child's account is activated.

For children 13–16 in the European Union and United Kingdom, we apply GDPR-K and the UK Children's Code, requiring either parental consent or, where permitted by local law, the child's own consent with appropriate safeguards.

### What Parents Can Do

Parents and guardians have the right to:

- **Review** all personal information we have collected about their child, at any time, by emailing privacy@promptcraft.academy.
- **Correct** any inaccurate information.
- **Delete** their child's account and all associated data (see Section 7).
- **Withdraw consent** and close the account at any time, with no penalty.
- **Opt out** of weekly progress digest emails at any time via the parent dashboard or by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.
- **Restrict** data processing beyond what is strictly necessary for the App to function.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@promptcraft.academy. We will respond within **30 days** (or sooner, as required by applicable law).

## 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share data with third parties **only** in the following limited circumstances, and only to the extent necessary:

| Third Party | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---|---|---|
| **AI Provider** (Anthropic / Groq) | Generating educational AI responses | Child's anonymised prompt text only. No name, email, or account data is shared. |
| **Cloud Infrastructure** (AWS) | Secure hosting and data storage | Encrypted account and usage data. AWS is SOC 2 Type II certified. |
| **Email Provider** (SendGrid) | Sending progress digests and account emails | Parent email address only. |
| **Payment Processor** (Apple / Google) | Processing subscription payments | Handled entirely by App Store / Google Play. We receive only a transaction confirmation. |
| **Crash Reporting** (Sentry) | Detecting and fixing technical errors | Anonymised error logs and device metadata. No personal data. |

We do **not** share data with:
- Advertisers or advertising networks
- Data brokers
- Social media platforms
- Analytics companies that profile individuals
- Any other third party for commercial purposes

In the event of a business transfer (such as a merger or acquisition), we will notify all users and obtain fresh consent before any data is transferred to a new entity under a different privacy policy.

---

## 6. Data Security

We take the security of children's data very seriously. Our security measures include:

- **Encryption in transit:** All data transferred between the App and our servers uses TLS 1.2 or higher.
- **Encryption at rest:** All stored personal data is encrypted using AES-256.
- **Access controls:** Only a small number of authorised engineers can access production data, under strict audit logging.
- **SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure:** Our hosting provider is independently audited for security compliance.
- **Regular security testing:** We conduct penetration testing and security reviews at least annually.
- **Breach notification:** In the event of a data breach affecting personal information, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities within the timeframes required by applicable law (72 hours under GDPR).

No system is 100% secure. If you have concerns about the security of your child's data, please contact us immediately at security@promptcraft.academy.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

### Retention Periods

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Parent account information | Until account deletion, plus 30 days |
| Child profile data | Until account deletion, plus 30 days |
| AI prompt history | 30 days from creation |
| Progress and achievement data | Until account deletion |
| Payment records | 7 years (legal requirement) |
| Anonymised usage analytics | Up to 2 years (no personal identifiers) |
| Crash logs | 90 days |

### How to Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your child's account and all associated personal data:

1. Open the App and go to **Parent Dashboard → Settings → Delete Account**, or
2. Email privacy@promptcraft.academy from the email address associated with your account.

We will complete the deletion within **30 days** and send you a confirmation. Anonymised, aggregated analytics that cannot identify your child will not be deleted as they do not constitute personal data.

## 8. No Advertising

PromptCraft Academy contains **no advertising of any kind.** We do not:

- Display third-party advertisements.
- Use advertising SDKs or tracking pixels.
- Collect advertising identifiers (IDFA or GAID).
- Build advertising profiles based on children's behaviour.
- Share data with advertising networks.

Our only revenue comes from subscriptions paid by parents. This will never change.

## 9. Push Notifications

With your permission, the App may send push notifications to remind children about their learning streak or notify parents of weekly progress. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or in the App's notification preferences.

We do not send marketing or promotional notifications to children.

For users in the EU and UK, our legal bases for processing personal data are set out in Section 3 above. In addition:
