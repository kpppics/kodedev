Our story

A family business,
15 years in the making.

Brow Arc was founded in 2009 by the Amiri family, who opened their very first threading salon in Santa Rosa, California. What began with a single chair has grown into more than thirty salons across California, Washington, Colorado and Florida.

Our mission has never changed — to exceed every client's expectations with the best in beauty services, retail products, customer service and value. We're here to help you feel confident and beautiful, and to make a little more good in the world while we do it.
