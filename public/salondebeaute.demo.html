01 The Master Stylist

Three decades behind the chair.

Allen has spent more than thirty years shaping hair — and shaping the standards of the people who work alongside him. Certified by L'Oréal Paris and certified in Brazilian Blowout, he built his reputation in the San Gabriel Valley before opening this atelier, where he once led a team of twenty-three stylists at a thriving Arcadia salon.

He works in Arabic, French and English, and treats every appointment the way the great houses treat couture: a careful read of the client, a precise plan, and a finish that holds long after you leave the chair.

The cut is only half of it — the rest is knowing exactly what suits you.
